"COE prices increased in the latest bidding exercise on April 17, with premiums for Category A cars, or those 1,600cc and below with horsepower not exceeding 130bhp, closing at S$94,010 (US$68,960), the highest since November 8, 2023. Premiums for larger cars in Category B rose to S$102,001 from S$101,334 while premiums in the open category COEs rose to S$103,249 from S$101,002. Premiums for motorcycles decreased slightly to S$9,990. The COE quota for the February to April quarter was raised by 2%. A total of 3,624 bids were received with 2,498 COEs available."
