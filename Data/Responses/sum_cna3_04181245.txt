"COE premiums in Singapore closed mostly higher in the latest bidding exercise, with Category A premiums, for smaller cars, exceeding SGD 90,000 for the first time in over five months. Premiums for larger and more powerful cars in Category B also rose. Open category COEs, suitable for any vehicle type but mostly used for larger cars, also increased. There was a total of 3,624 bids, with 2,498 COEs available."
